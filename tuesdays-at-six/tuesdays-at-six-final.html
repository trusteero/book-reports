Tuesdays at Six by Eero Get Lost

A Second-Chance Friends-to-Lovers Romance

Emma, a widowed engineer, returns to her late father's horse farm in northern England and reconnects with Luke, her first love and local farrier. Their structured friends-with-benefits arrangement on 'Tuesdays at six' evolves into genuine intimacy as they navigate grief, homecoming, and rekindled first love within a close-knit rural community. A high-heat, slow-burn reunion romance set against the backdrop of working farm life and emotional healing.

Unique Positioning

Tuesdays at Six occupies a unique space as a high-heat contemporary romance that authentically portrays grief, healing, and the evolution of structured intimacy into genuine love. Unlike many romance novels that focus primarily on the romantic relationship, this story balances explicit sexual content with meaningful emotional development and realistic portrayals of life after loss. The rural English setting and farm life backdrop provide atmospheric richness while the 'Tuesdays at six' structure offers a fresh approach to friends-to-lovers dynamics.
