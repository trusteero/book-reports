The Everlasting Gift by Wren Valentino

A Contemporary Romance of Homecoming, Renewal, and Second Chances

The Everlasting Gift is a heartfelt contemporary romance set in a small town during the holiday season, centering on themes of resilience, creative renewal, and the power of community. At its core is a second-chance love story that blends the warmth of festive tradition with the stakes of saving a local school through the transformative power of art. Its narrative weaves together the emotional arcs of homecoming, mentorship, and redemption, offering an uplifting reading experience that is both intimate and resonant for a wide audience.

Unique Positioning

The Everlasting Gift occupies a unique space as a holiday-themed contemporary romance that authentically portrays the challenges facing small-town schools and the power of arts education to unite communities. Unlike many holiday romances that focus primarily on the romantic relationship, this story balances romance with meaningful community engagement and realistic portrayals of educational advocacy.

Back Cover Blurb

When Sharleen returns to her small hometown for the holidays, she never expected to find herself at the center of a campaign to save the local school's arts program. With the help of an old friend and the support of a community that refuses to give up, she discovers that sometimes the most meaningful gifts are the ones we give to others. A heartwarming story of second chances, community spirit, and the magic that happens when people come together for a common cause.
